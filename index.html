About IDR

Built by industry.
Designed for the field.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment organisation for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

Through our partnership with a leading UK university, we combine academic depth with live project experience, producing practitioners who are deployment-ready from day one. IDR does not just train — we place, support and develop talent across the full risk lifecycle.

Our Model
Academic Partnership
Curriculum co-designed with a UK university partner, grounding every module in peer-reviewed methodology and current regulatory requirements.
Industry Practice
Live project engagements, real client environments and practitioner mentors ensure graduates are ready for the field, not just the classroom.
Regulated Sectors
Specialising in financial services, critical infrastructure and high-consequence environments where digital risk is mission-critical and failure is not an option.

Community

Who we serve.

IDR builds a practitioner community for those entering, advancing in, or leading digital and cyber risk functions. Whether you are a student stepping into the field or a seasoned professional seeking structured upskilling, IDR's programmes are designed around your context.

Our sectors of focus include financial services, critical national infrastructure, healthcare, energy and public sector — environments where digital risk is regulatory, operational and reputational.
